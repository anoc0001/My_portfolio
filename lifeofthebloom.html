THE PHILOSOPHY OF THE BLOOM

"Flowers are a guest in your home. We believe in appreciating the presence of a bloom rather than its longevity. Enjoy the moment while it lasts."

01 / ARRIVAL

THE FIRST BREATH

Cut stems at a 45-degree angle. This opens the veins, allowing them to drink deeply after arrival. Water is life—don't let them wait.

02 / NOURISHMENT

BLOOM FOOD

Use the provided packet. It contains the sugar, acid, and nutrients needed to help the flower open to its fullest potential.
